Explore The Greated Summer Collection

  • Summer fragrances are in stinctively light and fresh.
  • They are designed to adapt to high temperatures and suggest a refreshing sensation.
  • Fruity, floral and “aquatic” notes are often chosen for summer scents.
  • If you’re not brave enough to opt for fruity and exotic fragrances during the rest of the year, now’s the time to take the plunge!
  • Not a fan? Opt for invigorating citrus top notes and floral base notes for a stimulating effect.
  • It’s not always easy to choose a perfume and it’s just as complicated to pick the right seasonal fragrance.
  • Not sure how to choose a perfume for summer?
  • Let your intuition guide you as you find the ultimate carefree yet seductive fragrance: you’ll be irresistible this summer!

Explore The Greated Rainy Collection

  • The best monsoon fragrances are light and refreshing, such citrus, floral, or aquatic.
  • These include lemon, jasmine, and ocean breeze notes, which complement the damp atmosphere while providing a fresh, clean fragrance that is not overpowering.
  • Choose a perfume with earthy and green notes to evoke the freshness of wet leaves and the scent of petrichor, the pleasant aroma released when it rains on the ground.
  • Look for scents that contain ingredients like vetiver, moss, patchouli, or fresh grass because they capture the essence of outdoor rain.

Explore The Greated Winter Collection

  • During this season, our olfactory receptors are less sensitive, and fragrances tend to linger longer on the skin.
  • This is the time to embrace richer, deeper, and more complex scents.
  • Woody notes such as sandalwood, cedar, and patchouli provide a warm and cozy aura.
  • They will therefore have an excellent hold on clothing, like on scarves or sweaters.
  • Ingredients to look out for are leather notes, benzoin, honey, tonka bean and all kind of spices like cinnamon and cardamom.

Explore The Greated Night Out Collection

  • Choosing the right fragrance for a night out can make a lasting impression.
  • The scent you wear can enhance your overall presence and leave a memorable mark on those around you.
  • Evening perfumes often feature rich, warm, and sensual notes.
  • These include musk, vanilla, exotic florals, or deep woody scents.
  • These fragrances are more intense and have a longer-lasting scent trail, making them perfect for the night when our sense of smell is more acute.
