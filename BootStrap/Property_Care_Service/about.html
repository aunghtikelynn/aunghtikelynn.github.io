About ZKKB Property Care Service

Built by Hands. Trusted by Homeowners.

Founded in 2022, ZKKB Property Care Service was born out of a genuine passion for craftsmanship. We aren't just contractors; we are a dedicated team of builders who have constructed our own homes from the ground up. That hands-on journey taught us everything there is to know about a home’s structure, its quirks, and the care it requires. Today, we bring that same level of personal dedication and professional expertise to every project we touch. Whether it's a minor repair or a major renovation, we treat your home with the same respect and attention to detail as if it were our own.

Our Specialized Services

We provide a comprehensive range of interior and exterior solutions designed to keep your property in peak condition:

  • Interior & Exterior Renovation : Full-scale transformations to breathe new life into your living spaces.
  • Drywall & Painting : Professional finishes, seamless repairs, and fresh coats that redefine your home’s aesthetic.
  • Plumbing Fixture Upgrades : Modernizing your kitchen and bathroom with efficient, high-quality installations.
  • Electrical Fixture Upgrades : Enhancing your home’s lighting and safety with professional electrical updates.
  • AC Maintenance : Keeping your environment comfortable with reliable cooling system servicing.
